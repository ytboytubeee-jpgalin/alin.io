Planet Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our Solar System and is famous for its powerful storms and colourful cloud bands.

About Jupiter

Jupiter is a gas giant made mostly of hydrogen and helium. It has no solid surface like Earth. Its most famous feature is the Great Red Spot, a huge storm that has existed for hundreds of years. Jupiter also has many moons, including Ganymede, the largest moon in the Solar System.

Interesting Facts

Largest Planet

Jupiter is more than 11 times wider than Earth.

Great Red Spot

The Great Red Spot is a giant storm larger than Earth.

Many Moons

Jupiter has dozens of moons orbiting around it.
