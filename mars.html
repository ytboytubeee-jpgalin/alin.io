Planet Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet and is one of the most explored planets in our Solar System.

About Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is often called the Red Planet because of its reddish appearance caused by iron oxide on its surface. Scientists believe Mars may once have had water and conditions suitable for life.

Interesting Facts

Distance From Sun

Approximately 228 million km.

Length of Day

A day on Mars lasts 24.6 hours.

Moons

Mars has two moons named Phobos and Deimos.
