About BigPlanetarium

Inspiring curiosity and learning through exciting space education and interactive experiences.

Who We Are

BigPlanetarium is a Bristol-based educational organisation focused on teaching people about space, planets, galaxies, and the wonders of the universe. We aim to make science accessible, engaging, and enjoyable for visitors of all ages and abilities.

Accessibility Features

High contrast colours and readable fonts improve visibility for users.

The website adapts to mobile phones, tablets, and desktop devices.

Accessible Navigation

Keyboard-friendly navigation and semantic HTML improve usability.
