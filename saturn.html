Planet Saturn

Saturn is one of the most beautiful planets in the Solar System because of its bright and unique ring system.

About Saturn

Saturn is a gas giant and the sixth planet from the Sun. It is best known for its large ring system, which is made from ice, dust, and rock particles. Saturn also has many moons, including Titan, which is one of the most interesting moons for space scientists.

Interesting Facts

Famous Rings

Saturn’s rings are made of ice and rock particles.

Gas Giant

Saturn is mostly made of hydrogen and helium gas.

Titan Moon

Titan is Saturn’s largest moon and has a thick atmosphere.
